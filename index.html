About us

Zimkitha Macengulashe Zilo

started reading at a very early age. She says reading has made her a more aware person, boosted her confidence levels, and literally made her an encyclopedia of random facts. Originally from the Free State, she moved to Cape Town where she resided in Khayelitsha.

The Bookniks

Seeing children loitering in the streets with nothing to do and the streets flooded with alcohol bottles and cigarette stompies, she would envision the whole streets flooded with booksand children reading instead of finishing off alcohol bottles and pretending to smoke. So as a way to bring her imaginary book flooding, she has children's book clubs around Khayelitsha and also reads in the Pediatric ward of Khayelitsha District Hospital.She gets her books from the center of books and so far, she hasn't had a shortage of books which is "amazing" in her own words.
